Italy

Bell'Italia!

Italy is one of the larger countries in Europe. It shares borders with France, Switzerland, Austria, Slovenia, San Marino and Vatican City. It was established by the Roman Empire and carried on by the Roman Catholic Church. Some of the world's iconic buildings and priceless art can be found in Italy.

Italy is famous for its delicious cuisine, its trendy fashions, luxury sports cars and motorcycles, diverse regional cultures and dialects, as well as for its various sceneries from the seas to the Alps.
